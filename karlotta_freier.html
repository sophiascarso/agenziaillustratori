Karlotta Freier

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration.

She has taught illustration at the China Academy of Art and spoken as a guest speaker at universities across the United States. Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of solo and group exhibitions, as well as festivals worldwide.

Her achievements have been recognized by the Art Directors Club, which awarded her a Cube in 2018 and a Pencil for Best Illustration Portfolio in 2021.
