About Us

Moře is a creative agency founded by Sophia Scarso Space in different fields. In particular, he specializes in advertising and promotional activities.

Moře’s goal is to be able to create a very specific identity for brands, individuals and companies, developing logos, graphics and animated content. From particular color choices to unique caricatures, Moře is ready to use any solution to support and guide its customers.

Many of Moře's artists have already worked with Italian publishing houses, standing out for their styles and mastery of different genres and techniques. Furthermore, Moře is actively involved in following its artists in the search for jobs and collaborations, and also in maintaining active contacts with the most important realities of Italian and international publishing.
